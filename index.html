About ITH Sport

Built on Precision
& Reliability

ITH Sport is the garment manufacturing division of Ideal Trend Hungary. We produce garments at scale for international brands, retailers and sourcing companies across every product category.

Our two facilities in Zalaegerszeg and Békés form the core of production, with all key work kept in-house under tight quality control. When volumes demand it, trusted partners let us scale further — so large orders and tighter deadlines are met without compromise.

Quality & Precision First

Scalable Production Capacity

EU-Based, Duty-Free Delivery

About ITH Marine

Precision Marine
Upholstery Manufacturing

ITH Marine is the marine upholstery-cover division of Ideal Trend Hungary. Building on decades of textile-industry and sewing expertise, we manufacture custom marine upholstery covers for yachts and luxury vessels.

All cutting and sewing is carried out in-house at our Hungarian facilities, ensuring full control over quality. We manufacture new covers to match your existing ones — you send us the original cover (and foam, if it also needs replacing), and we produce an exact new set. We don't carry out on-board fitting, but transport can be arranged on request for an additional fee.

Upholstery Cover Manufacturing — 100% In-House
All cutting, sewing and finishing done by our own team. Foam replacement available where needed.
Premium Yacht Experience
Yachts, catamarans and luxury vessels of all types.
EU-Based, Duty-Free Delivery
Fast, duty-free shipping to clients and marinas across Europe.

About ITH Event

Branded Event
Textile Products

ITH Event is the event textile division of Ideal Trend Hungary. We produce custom branded textile products for sport and outdoor events — from beanbags to tent covers, sunbeds, barriers and pillows.

Products are manufactured in-house at our Hungarian facilities, with the capacity to scale for large events and tight timelines. You simply send us the artwork — the logo or design to be applied — no sample is needed. We deliver the finished products ready to use; on-site setup is not part of our service, and delivery can be arranged on request. We work with event organisers, sports federations and corporate clients across Europe.

Full Custom Branding
Logo, sponsor marks and corporate identity on every product — to your guidelines.
Scalable for Big Events
In-house production with the capacity to scale and meet large event volumes on time.
EU-Based, Duty-Free Delivery
Fast shipping across Europe with no import duties.
